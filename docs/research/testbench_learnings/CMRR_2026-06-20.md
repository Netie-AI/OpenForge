1. Is CMRR a direct `vdb(vout)` read like PSRR, or a ratio `Ad/Acm`?

3. Why does W3 move PSRR 20→83 dB but CMRR stays ~172 dB flat?
4. Is 172 dB credible against RS321 typ 80 dB?
5. Should comparator/vref get CMRR benches?

## dv-verifier judgment (extract essentials)

1. **Normalization:** CM `ac 0.1` vs diff `ac 1` → add 20 dB correction or unify to `ac 1`.
2. **Credibility:** Even corrected ~152 dB >> 80 dB → label `unverified` for datasheet compare; open-loop + no RL.
3. **Causal sanity:** W3 flat on CMRR vs strong on PSRR → bench may not exercise mirror path; `relative_only` until fixed.
4. **Blind spot:** CMRR bench-only + `meets_all=True` repeats PSRR pattern — do not trust envelope pass.
5. **Commands that matter:** `verify_cmrr.py`, `diag_opamp_cmrr_breakdown.py`, raw `aol100`/`acm_db` extraction.
